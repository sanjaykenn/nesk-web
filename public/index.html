NESK - NES Emulator written in Rust

Since cartridges have their own internal chipset, which varies from game to game, cartridges have to be emulated as well. Currently only games seen here are supported, but more will be added in the future. To play a game, you need to load an NES ROM file. Due to copyright restrictions, I can't share any of those games.
